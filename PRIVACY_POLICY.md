# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 27, 2026

## Introduction

Read & Learn Chinese ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Articles**: Chinese text articles you create or import
- **Reading Progress**: Your reading position and completion status
- **Vocabulary**: Words you mark as known or unknown
- **Tags**: Custom tags you create for organizing vocabulary

### Data from Device Features
- **Camera**: Used only for scanning text from images (optional feature)
- **Photo Library**: Used only for importing images containing text (optional feature)
- **Documents**: Used only for importing PDF/TXT files (optional feature)

## How We Use Your Information

All data is stored **locally on your device** using:
- AsyncStorage for articles and settings
- SQLite database for dictionary and vocabulary tracking

We do **NOT**:
- Upload your data to any server
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Track you across other apps or websites

## Data Storage

Your data remains on your device until:
- You delete the app
- You manually delete content within the app
- You use the data export feature (if available)

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- View all stored articles
- Delete individual articles
- Reset vocabulary tracking
- Export your data (future feature)

## Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
